# NIGEL — DO THIS

## Enter forfeit / walkover matches as a 6-0 6-0 score

When a match is a no-show / forfeit, **don't leave it with an empty score.**
Record it as a normal completed result with the winner taking both sets **6-0, 6-0**:

- Status: **completed** (not "forfeit" with blank scores)
- Sets: **6-0, 6-0** in favour of the team that showed up / won
- Winner: the team that gets the walkover

### Why
The standings are calculated from the recorded set scores. A match left as a
forfeit with no scores counts as **0 sets won**, so:
- the win can get dropped from the table entirely, and
- "Sets Won" (the tiebreaker after points) comes out wrong.

Entering 6-0 6-0 gives the winners their **3 points + 2 sets won**, which is what
a walkover should award.

### Example that bit us
Group B match #746 — Gil/Shogo beat Kino/Andy — was saved as a forfeit with no
scores, so it disappeared from the standings until the 6-0 6-0 was supplied
manually. Enter the score directly and this won't happen.
